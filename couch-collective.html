Community Standards

A curated, protected space.


Membership Criteria

This membership is intentionally curated to maintain a safe and aligned environment. Members must:

  • Identify as a Black woman
  • Align with the values of The Sisters' Couch
  • Be committed to contributing to a respectful and supportive space
Community Guidelines

To protect the integrity of the space, members agree to:

  • Respect confidentiality and privacy
  • Contribute to a calm and restorative environment
  • Engage with care, consent, and mutual respect
