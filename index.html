About the Hub

A space built for healers

The Sisters' Couch has spent over eight years building a trusted community of Black women committed to rest, healing, and connection. The Healing Hub is our permanent home — a beautiful, intentional space where practitioners and clients alike can exhale.

We are looking for wellness specialists who share our values and want to build their practice inside a space that already has community roots, warmth, and purpose behind it.

This is more than a suite rental. This is an invitation to be part of something that matters.

The Wellness Suite
